Apple Vs. Android

In last years battle of the flagship electronics Apple & Samsung were neck and neck as far as competitiveness is concerned. The iPhone 11 Pro Max and Samsung Galaxy S 10+ were very formidable in sales and what they each offered consumers. I for one am a hardcore Apple fanboy. I've bought my own iPhones since I was 15 and have been purchasing then latest ever since. I must admit I am quite biased when it comes to functionality and OS, however I must also admit that Samsung is bringing the heat and stepping up with additional features and must have quality of life updates. Competition is always great and I wish the best to both companies for the sake of the consumers!

iPhone

The design of the iPhone has an iconic design that is beloved worldwide. This is most likely one of the reasons why the design has a very hard time changing only ever so slightly over the last 5 years. Design aside, the functionality of the device is what will take the cake for most users. The simplicity of syncing your phone with your contacts and emails really gives the consumer a peace of mind when utilizing this device. The sleekness and longevity of this device to last year after year is also another fine selling point with guaranteed updates for 5 years after your purchase. When going out on a limb for a $$$ costly device you want to feel assured you are making the right choice without putting a pit in your gut. The iPhone will always be a safe bet for me. Anyone else agree with this perspective?

Galaxy

The Samsung Galaxy phones have been a very prominent and pivitol competitor to the iPhone. I will always say competition is great for business as it forces other companies to step their game up. Some would disagree with that statement, but without that competitive spirit there is no will for advancemment. Businesses without a will to advance will ultimately become complacement and be overtaken by another hard working company with what they are lacking! So far for iPhone and Galaxy phones that will is there and it will thrust our nation forward!
