Reading to Enrich Your Soul

Hello and welcome to the beginning of a brighter future. My name is Imani and I want to shed some light on a very valuable life choice. Reading is an invaluable resource that is used to expand the horizon of all who take heed and grasp onto this fountain of youth.

Healthly Habits

In the list provided below you will encounter many healthy habits that you can start utilizing today!

  • Set Times
  • Always Carry A Book
  • Make A List
  • Find A Quiet Place
  • Trade TV/Internet Time For Reading
  • Read To Your Children
  • Keep A Book Log
  • Go To Used Book Shops
  • Have A Library Day
  • Read A Variety Of Books That Peak Your Interest
  • Make Reading Enjoyable With Your Favorite Bevarage/Snack
  • Blog About Your Progress
  • Set High Goals
  • Create A Reading Hour Or A Reading Day
These options are guaranteed to curate an exceptional reader. One great benefit I personally experience has to do with an enhanced perception on many topics that actually coordinate with what i'm currently reading. Reading gives the brain pause and allows it to flex it's critical thinking skills and allow the pressures of life to decompress as you're swept away into a serene ocean of knowledge and power.

Knowledge Is Power! Use It Wisely!

You may think this quote is just a cheesy cliche, but if that were the case why then are the world's elites constantly found engaged in reading day in and day out? Important people all around the world are feeding their brains with knowledge which in turn allows them to operate from a more informed position resulting in better outcomes. Billionaires don't become billionaires let alone stay billionaires without reading about and being taught about financial literacy and real-world examples on how to conduct their business. Today we live in a technology age and knowledge is at our fingertips. Anything you could dream of can be searched and researched for the betterment of any and all lives.

More Is Required Of You

In Todays society more is required of you than ever before. Reading cannot be replaced with watching TV or browsing the internet for cute cat pics.
